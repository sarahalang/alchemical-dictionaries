What
----

Data source: the reviewed extraction dataframe supplied to the script; metrics also draw on the TEI dictionary when available.

How
---
This table is assembled inside `plots_headword_letter_coverage` by reshaping and aggregating the pandas dataframe and exporting the result with `DataFrame.to_csv`, as shown in the snippet below.
