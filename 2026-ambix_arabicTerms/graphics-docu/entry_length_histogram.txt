What
----
Distribution of entry lengths measured in word tokens across the full Ruland dictionary. Highlights the uneven mix of very short and very long entries.

Data source: the reviewed extraction dataframe supplied to the script; metrics also draw on the TEI dictionary when available.

How
---
This figure is generated inside `plots_entry_length_overview` by preparing the relevant pandas data structures and rendering the visual with seaborn/matplotlib, as illustrated in the code snippet below.

Code
----
    fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(10, 5))
    sns.histplot(entries["word_count"], bins=60, color="#4C78A8", ax=ax)
    ax.set_title("Ruland Entry Word Counts (All Entries)")
    ax.set_xlabel("entry length (words)")
    ax.set_ylabel("entries")
    ax.set_yscale("linear")
    save_plot_with_explanation(
        fig,
        os.path.join(outdir, "entry_length_histogram.png"),
        "Distribution of entry lengths measured in word tokens across the full Ruland dictionary."
        " Highlights the uneven mix of very short and very long entries.",
    )
